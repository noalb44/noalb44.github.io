My Favorite Travel Destination: Costa Rica

In high school my family and I took a trip to Costa Rica. For the first three days of the trip we stayed in a hotel near the beach and the last four we traveled inland to the Foruna Volcano. We went on a hike through the rainforest and tried the local food.
